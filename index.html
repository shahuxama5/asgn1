Welcome To UBIT Grammar School

UBIT Grammar School, is an independent, English-medium school in Karachi, Pakistan. It is a coeducational day school. Over the last three decades, the school has been offering O Levels held under the Cambridge International Examinations and Secondary School Certificate held under the Board of Secondary Education, Karachi.
